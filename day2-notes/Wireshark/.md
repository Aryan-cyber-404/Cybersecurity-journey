### Wirshark intro:-
I opened the wireshark applications and clicked the capture button to start capture.
with option of eth0 cause i am doing this over ethernet connection.
Next i opened the terminal and typed "ping google,com" 
several rows are showing on the wireshark interface , those are the request and reply of datapackets with google.com

After that i typed ICMP on filter bar it showes me the source ip and destination ip which is google
 
Then i removed icmp and typed dns on filter bar , i saw different ip which was not in icmp ..,  i got to know that system is asking what is the address of google.com through dns packets
