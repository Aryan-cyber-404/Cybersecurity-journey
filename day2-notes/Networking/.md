### Networking Deeper :-

#### local ip 
after typing "ip a" , under the "enp0s3" section the address attached to inet is the local ip address.

#### loopback address
it comes under the "lo" section after typing "ip a" in teminal . it is basically 127.0.0.1 in every devices.

#### gateway 
When we type "ip route" ,there will be like "default via [ip address] dev enp0s3" . the ip address here is the gateway.
