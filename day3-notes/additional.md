### Q. there are two commands one is "ping 127.0.0.1" and another is "ping google.com" .. So what difference exists between them ?

ping 127.0.0.1 :- here we send traffic requests to 127.0.0.1 which basically loopback address and to check connectivity/connection reliabity of device .
    it does not require network connection

ping google.com :- here we send traffic requests to the host of google.com over the internet.
    it checks whether my internet connection or dns working.
    it requires active internet connection
