### Why are failed login attempts important in cybersecurity ?
    -> if there are one or two failed attempts it might be the user's fault of entering wrong passowrd or wrong commands .
    But
    -> if there is multiple failed attempts in short time like "100 failed attempts in one minute" it leads to suspicious activity. 
        Then analysts have to investigate the user ,  they check whether it is required for the company or system or whether it is valid or not . 
        it can lead to brute force attack , weak password attacks etc..
